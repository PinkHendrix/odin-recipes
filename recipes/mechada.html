Carne Mechada!

The Best Shredded Beef in the World: Carne Mechada.

From scratch, you will fall in love with this dish.

Ingredients
  • Crock Pot
  • Chuck Roast
  • Mix of Herbs and Spices
    • Kosher Salt
    • Black Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
  • Homemade Tomato Sauce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Brown Sugar
  • Beer

Steps
  1. Season the Roast
  2. Add all Ingredients into the Pot
  3. Set the Pot to cook on 'Pressure Cook' (100 minutes)
  4. Once cooked remove, place on a dish and shred the roast with a pair of forks
  5. Pour some liquid from pot over the now shredded meat
  6. Serve and enjoy, to add to your arepa check out our Arepa recipe
  7. That's it, voila!
