Arepa!

The Best Arepa in the World: Pabellon Criollo.

From VZ to the world, one of many contributions to the culinary field.

Warning: May cause you to permanently emigrate to VZ to eat this delicatessen everyday for the rest of your life.

Ingredients
  • Flat Skillet
  • Harina Pan Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Carne Mechada, aka shredded beef
  • Fried Ripe Plantains, aka Tajadas
  • Black Beans, aka Caraotas Negras
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps
  1. Mix half the bag of dough with water and salt (7 minutes)
  2. Roll into palm sized-balls (5 minutes)
  3. Flatten the balls and place on skillet
  4. Place skillet on stovetop at Medium heat (10 minutes)
  5. Preheat small oven to 375 Fahrenheit
  6. Flip arepa and cook on other side (5 minutes)
  7. For extra crispy outside spread on baking sheet
  8. Transfer to oven and bake (10 minutes)
  9. Toast for the last touch (5 minutes)
  10. Remove and cut open to fill with the shredded beef, plantains, butter and black beans, voila!
