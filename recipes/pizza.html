Pizza!

The Best Pizza in the World: The Pan Supreme Pizza.

From scratch you would have to choose the dough and place it into a pan until it rises.

Proceed to add the cheese and assorted toppings, we recommend a mix of meats and veggies.

Ingredients
  • Cast Iron Skillet
  • Rising Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Homemade Tomato Sauce
  • Oregano
  • Assorted Meats
  • Assorted Veggies

Steps
  1. Make the dough (15 minutes)
  2. Knead the dough (8 minutes)
  3. Proof the dough (45 minutes)
  4. Press and Proof again (45 minutes)
  5. Preheat the oven to 475 Fahrenheit
  6. Add all your toppings
  7. Place skillet on stovetop burner on high heat (2 minutes)
  8. Transfer to oven and bake (17 minutes)
  9. Remove and slice to serve, voila!
